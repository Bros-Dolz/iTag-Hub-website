What it does

Everything a lost-item app should be

Reliable where it counts, honest about its limits, and private by default.

Ring your tag

Make your tag beep straight from the app so you can find it fast — even across the room.

Warmer / colder Find Mode

A live proximity radar reads the signal and guides you through the last few steps.

Alerts you can trust

Out-of-range warnings are tuned with hysteresis to ignore brief dips — no false alarms.

Your button, your rules

One press or two triggers phone actions: play a sound, log a location, or snap a photo.

Works with any tag

Pairs with generic, no-brand BLE tracker tags. Not locked to a single manufacturer.

Private by design

No account, no cloud, no analytics. Your tags, locations, and photos never leave the phone.

Bluetooth, not GPS

iTag Hub works within Bluetooth range — about 10–30 metres. It's an item finder that helps you locate things nearby, not a GPS tracker, and it never pretends to be one.
