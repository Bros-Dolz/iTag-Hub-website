What it does

Built to help you find what matters

Built for quick finds, clear guidance, and privacy that does not get in the way.

Ring your tag

Make your tag beep straight from the app so you can find it fast, even across the room.

Warmer / colder find mode

A live proximity radar reads the signal and guides you through the last few steps.

Alerts you can trust

Out-of-range warnings use hysteresis to ignore brief dips, so you avoid noisy false alarms.

Your button, your rules

One press or two can trigger phone actions like playing a sound, logging a location, or snapping a photo.

Works with any tag

Works with generic BLE tracker tags, so you are not locked into a single manufacturer.

Private by design

No account, no cloud, no analytics. Your tags, locations, and photos stay on the phone.

Bluetooth, not GPS

iTag Hub works within Bluetooth range, about 10 to 30 metres. It is designed to help you find things nearby, not to act like a GPS tracker.
